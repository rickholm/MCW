Only 5 minutes from the freeway, from the north, take exit 116 from Interstate 5 and turn left and travel aproximately 3 miles. From the south, take exit 114 and turn right. Go thru the light and continue on to Kuhlman road and turn left. At the stop sign on Old Pacific Hiway, turn right on Old Pacific Hiway and travel about half a mile.


The winery is located on the right (West) side of Old Pacific Hiway.

Hours: open Saturdays and Sundays from noon to 5:00.
